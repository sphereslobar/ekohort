Welcome to eKohort
Maternal Health Management System

eKohort is a web-based application for managing maternal health records. The system helps healthcare providers track and manage:

  • Patient Identity Information
  • Medical Examinations
  • Laboratory Results
  • Delivery Information
  • Postpartum Care
  • Antenatal Care (ANC)

To get started:

  1. Sign in using your Google account (top right corner)
  2. Select a module from the navigation menu
  3. Start managing patient records
All data is stored securely in Google Sheets, ensuring easy access and backup.
